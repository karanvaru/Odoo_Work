Additional Fees on Purchase Order Line

1. Go to Purchase => Order => Requests For Quotation, Create Purchase Quotation.
2. Click on Button.
3. Add Additional Fee Line and Click on save.
4. Click on Confirm Order.
5. Click on Receive Products.
6. Click on Validate.
7. Click on Create Bill.
8. Additional Fees Line is added in Vendor Invoice Line.
