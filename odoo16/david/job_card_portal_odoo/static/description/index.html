Job Card Portal Odoo App

This app allows your customer to view their job card details on the portal of your website and do a signature on the portal on the job card form.

Main Features:

  • Allow your customers to login into the portal and view their job card details.
  • Customers can accept and sign the job card from the portal.
  • You can see the signature of the customer in the PDF report of the job card.
  • Customers can send messages from the portal.
  • For more details please check the video and check the below screenshots.
