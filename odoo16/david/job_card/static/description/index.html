Job Card App in Odoo for Workshops / Factory / Any Technical / Repair Shops / Industry


Job Card App in Odoo using Project Management App

Job Card for Worker / Technician / Internal Users / Machine Operators / Supervisors.

This app allows your users to have a job card feature as shown in the below screenshots with all the details related to that job along with instructions to complete a job.


Your Worker / Technician / Users / Machine Operator / All Types of Job Workers can access job cards in Odoo and see details and instructions. They can also fill the Timesheet(s) in the time card tab available on the Job card.


Main Features:

  • Job card form and list view for your users/operators/supervisors/technician. Allow you to create and manage job cards with things needed to execute jobs.
  • Allow your manager to set up a Workshop Teams under configuration and also allow them to set it on a job card form.
  • Allow your manager to set up a Quality Checklists under configuration and also allow them to set it on a job card form.
  • Allow user to set and manage job related instructions on the job card form.
  • Allow users to set and manage job-related cost sheets on the job card form.
  • Allow your user to choose job card reference on material requisition request.
  • Allow your user to create and manage material requisition for the job card.
  • For more details about requisition you can check https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/16.0/material_purchase_requisitions/
  • Allow you to add job invoice lines on the job card and then create an invoice based on it from job card.
  • Allow your users/workers to fill the timesheet(s) on the job card form.
  • Allow your user to print PDF report for job card and cost sheet from job card form as shown.
  • For the additional reports you can check and download/buy separately https://apps.odoo.com/apps/modules/16.0/job_card_report/
  • For more details please check the below screenshots and watch the video.

General Workflow:

  • The project manager/supervisor can configure the quality checklists and workshop team and instructions on the Job Card.
  • The project manager/supervisor can create and manage the Job Card.
  • Project user/job operator/technician can view/add/work with the instruction, cost sheet, material requisition, requisition line, job invoice line, and timesheet on the job card form.
  • The project manager can create and manage the customer invoice from the job invoice line on the job card form.
