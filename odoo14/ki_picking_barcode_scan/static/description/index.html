Search Product and Picking

  • Go to Inventory => Search Product and Picking.
  • Select Picking Search By Name and Product Search By Barcode.
  • Enter Picking Name and Product Barcode.
  • Click on Validate.
  • Your picking successfully validated.
  • Picking is validated.
  • Select Picking Search By Tracking Reference and Product Search By Internal Reference.
  • Enter Picking Tracking Reference and Product Internal Reference.
  • Click on Validate.
  • Picking is validated.
  • Go to Inventory => Search Picking.
  • Select Operation Type and Picking Search By.
  • Enter picking Name or Tracking Reference or AWB Number.
  • Enter Product Search By Lot Number.
  • Click On Validate.
  • The scanned product is validated.
  • Unscanned product becomes back order.
