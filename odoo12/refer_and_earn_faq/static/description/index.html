About FAQ's Project:-

FAQ is an online document that poses a series of common questions and answers on a specific topic. All supposed to be commonly asked in some context, and pertaining to a particular program.

With the help of FAQs, one can describe the features of a program in the form of questions and answers. So that the users can easily understand the benefits of the program.
