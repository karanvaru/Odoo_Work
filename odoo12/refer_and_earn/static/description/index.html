Odoo Refer And Earn Extension

You can easily manage your Referrals

INCREASE YOUR STORE SALES THROUGH A WORD OF MOUTH. BY ALLOWING PEOPLE TO SHARE THEIR REFERRAL CODE (or) LINK TO THEIR FRIENDS AND EARN REWARDS.

About This Extension

  • Refer us! To your connections (friend and family) and earn Rewards. Through our Refer and Earn program, you just need to share your auto-generated Unique Referral Code (Referral code will be generated automatically once you sign-up with us) with your connections. Once your referred connections join us using your referral code. You will be rewarded by us. You can even track your referrals and the reward earned in the real time via our personalized dashboard.

Benefits this Extension Allows

  • Easy to use
  • Earn Rewards and increase your store sales
  • Easy to track your Stats
  • Easy to Share Referral link in just a single click
  • Customisable Reward policies(either on Sign-up or after the first sale order)
  • Customisable Rewarding Policies (Either to both Referral and Referrer or anyone of them)
  • Unlimited Sharing of Referral Code
  • Auto-generated Personalised dashboard for your customer to track their reward stats
  • No, Expiration Date of referral code
  • There is no time limit on the reward
  • Once money is credited to the account can be used anytime in future
  • Open for all . Everyone can be invited who still have not joined your store
  • Real-time stats of earnings with details
  • Allows Admin to set the different Reward Points for Referral and Referrer
  • Allows admin to configure the Reward point value, like 1 Reward Point = 10$
  • Quick and Easy Installation

  • Full Admin Control
    • Can Confirm the Transactions manually or auto-approve
    • Define/Update welcome page Content
    • Full control on Refer and Earn Policies
    • Can track transaction history of every customer
  • Website View
  • Admin Side View
  • Configuration
