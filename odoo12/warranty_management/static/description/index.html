Odoo Website Warranty Management

Track warranty data and reports for your products in the Odoo backend.

The module allows you to include warranty for the products in the Odoo. It adds the warranty field in the product in the Odoo. The admin can add the duration for which the warranty is valid for a product after purchase.
The sales order will also show the start and end dates of the warranty for a product. Once the delivery is done in Odoo, an automatic mail is sent to the customer along with the details and terms of the warranty. The customers can also register their product with the Odoo.
The admin can also set up a reminder mail to be delivered to the customer to notify to extend the warranty of the product.

Features:

  • The admin can manage warranty details of the products directly in Odoo.
  • The product form enables adding the duration of warranty provided on the product post purchase.
  • The warranty details are automatically added in the Order as soon as the delivery is confirmed.
  • The admin can also check the warranty details in the picking generated for the order.
  • The customer receives an automatic mail once the delivery is done. The mail contains the details for warranty of the product.
  • The customers can also register their product(s) with the Odoo, at which point, the warranty registration gets automatically done in Odoo.
  • The customers can view and download the warranty details for a product through their Odoo website account.
  • The admin can set up a automatic reminder mail to be sent to the customer days or weeks before the warranty of his/her product expires.
  • The customers can renew the product’s warranty from their account.
  • Once the warranty is successfully extended, an automatic confirmation mail is sent to the customer.
